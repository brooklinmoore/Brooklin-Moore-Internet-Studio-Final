Monstera deliciosa

Tropical vining plant with fenestrated leaves. Beginner→Intermediate.

Care summary

Light
Bright indirect light.
Water
Water when top 2 inches of soil are dry; moderate humidity preferred.
Soil
Well-draining potting mix with perlite.
Difficulty
Beginner → Intermediate

Symptom checker

Describe the symptom or select common options — suggestions appear below.

Read care guide

Suggested actions

  • Check for overwatering: remove plant, inspect roots for rot.
  • Move to brighter indirect light if leaves are pale.
  • Trim brown edges; avoid fertilizers until recovery.
